OUR CHRIST JESUS 





love we expect and cherish we aspire.
Good we lust and care we sought.
Who ever thought love could be at the strangest places.
who would have imagined that the one despised by many would be the one waited for.





we were sold for nothing but he bought us dearly
Nobody could afford us,only God could.
Yes!,with the blood of his only son
he bought us back
he never bought us for any gains but for the sake of his love for us
